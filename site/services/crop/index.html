Crop Insurance

Crop protection for indoor and outdoor cannabis lifecycle stages.

Coverage Highlights

Your plants are your inventory, and losing a crop cycle to equipment failure, contamination, or natural disaster isn't abstract — it's catastrophic. Crop coverage for cannabis goes beyond basic plant value and follows your product through every stage of the lifecycle, from seedling to packaged finished stock ready for distribution.

  • Standard ISO Forms
  • Pesticide and Fertilizer built in
  • Seedlings, Vegetative, Harvest
  • Indoor and Outdoor
  • Goods in Process — Drying and Curing Stage
  • Finished Stock — Packaged Ready for Sale
  • Stated Value
  • Additional Insureds

All policies remain subject to underwriting and final carrier eligibility.
