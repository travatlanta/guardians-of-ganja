Products & Completed Ops

Coverage for product liability and post-sale exposure across cannabis operations.

Coverage Highlights

Once your product leaves the facility, your exposure doesn't stop. Products and completed operations coverage protects you from third-party claims arising from what you manufacture, distribute, or sell — including contamination events, product recall costs, and liability tied to labeling and pesticide use built directly into the policy form.

  • Limits up to $5m / $5m
  • Standard ISO Forms
  • Can be included in General Liability
  • $250,000 Product Withdrawal
  • Pesticide and Fertilizer built in
  • Additional Insureds

All policies remain subject to underwriting and final carrier eligibility.
