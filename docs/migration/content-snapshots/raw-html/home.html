Who we Are

As the cannabis industry continues to grow throughout the nation, so does the need for specialized insurance coverages. Being cannabis advocates and having first hand experience with the medical benefits, we recognize the industry is greatly underserved in many ways. With a team of cannabis professionals totaling over 20 years experience, our mission is to provide the highest quality customer service by working directly with the nations leading cannabis insurance carriers. We strive to bring our clients the most comprehensive coverage tailored for their unique business needs.

Here at Guardians of Ganja we believe in strong communication and attention to details. When you call, you will speak directly with Dalton or Jason. NO Transfers, NO holding for another representative, and NO long wait times! We are proud to be a professional resource for the cannabis industry, and are committed to providing a friendly, professional, and trustworthy service our clients deserve and count on!
