# Page Skeleton and Build Order

## Build Sequence
1. Home
2. Services Overview
3. Service Detail Template
4. Contact
5. Gallery
6. Privacy and Terms (if missing)

## Home Skeleton
- Hero with trust-forward value proposition
- Cannabis segment positioning block
- Coverage summary cards
- Social proof or expertise signal
- Conversion CTA block

## Services Overview Skeleton
- Intro summary
- Reusable coverage cards grid
- Why Guardians of Ganja differentiator
- Secondary conversion CTA

## Service Detail Skeleton (Reusable)
- Coverage headline and summary
- Typical risk scenarios
- Eligibility or underwriting notes
- FAQ short block
- Conversion CTA

## Contact Skeleton
- Quote-focused lead section
- Required form fields
- Validation and anti-spam controls
- Direct contact fallback details

## Gallery Skeleton
- Filterable media grid
- Optimized media delivery
- Caption and alt-text ready metadata
